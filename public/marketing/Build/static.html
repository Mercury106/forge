Why go static?

Static websites with Forge are the future

Sign Up for free Or Log In now

Static brings the thunder and Forge is as fast as lightning.

  1. Many of today's sites use slow programming languages and database calls on the server to compile and serve up your sites.
  2. But there's a secret to better performance.
  3. By taking your site static, you can be sure that your web pages will always be insanely fast. No servers or database queries to worry about.

Why is static a better way to build and host my website?

Speed Every time your visitors have to wait for a page to load, they're one click away from leaving. A static site can be served much, much quicker.

Control Take control over your markup and create and update it your way. Static sites are about the craftsman and their tools, not systems and data.

Robustness By decoupling your sites from servers and databases you're removing overhead and making your site as performant as it can be.
