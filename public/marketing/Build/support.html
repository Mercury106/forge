TurboJS tips

Put your Javascripts in the <head> element.

TurboJS is a brand-new javascript library developed in collaboration with Alex MacCaw. TurboJS is a unique new engine, designed to serve all your site's pages in the background - making your site super, duper, extra fast.

General tips

Concatenate your stylesheets and javascripts

Got a bunch of stylesheets? Put them all together to make them extra speedy. This means fewer HTTP requests, which means less overhead for your users, and faster-loading pages.

  <link href="normalize.css" />
  <link href="style.css" />
  <link href="pages.css" />
  <link href="buttons.css" />
 
  <link href="public.css" />

If you're using Hammer for Mac, remember to build with "Optimized" mode enabled.
